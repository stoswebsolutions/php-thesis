Submit your research article/manuscript in .DOC/.DOCX file format to us by mail or online submission.

  • Submit online by clicking here.
  • Submit by mail to editor@ijrar.org In the subject of the mail, write “Submission of Manuscript/Research Paper: Name of Paper”. Also mention paper category, title of the paper, full name of the author, designation, and contact number in the message body of the mail.

Next to submission

  • Please prepare your research paper/manuscript in IJRAR paper format (click here for download). Also, submitted papers MUST be written in English.
  • Our team will acknowledge your submission via email, and you will receive a unique registration ID for the submitted paper.
  • Authors can track their submitted paper status online by logging in to the AUTHOR'S HOME account. For login, authors must use their paper registration ID and corresponding author’s email ID.

Paper accept/reject notification

  • Our reviewer team will evaluate the submitted research paper and notify you whether the research paper is accepted or rejected.
  • Authors can also check the review result in their AUTHOR HOME account.
  • If the paper is accepted, authors must pay the publication fees.
  • If the paper is rejected, the author must make modifications as per the instructions provided by our reviewer committee.

Document submission

Once the publication fee has been paid, authors must submit the following documents in a single mail to editor@ijrar.org:

  • Undertaking by Author Form (scanned copy) - handwritten and signed by the author (Download).
  • Copyright transfer form (scanned copy) - handwritten and signed by the author (Download).
  • Identity proof of the corresponding author (scanned copy) (Election Card, PAN Card, College ID, Driving License).
  • Payment proof (PDF file or screenshot) including the paid amount, transaction ID, and date of payment.
  • Final manuscript/research paper (must be in .DOC format with the file name as your registration ID).

Paper publication

After receiving the above-mentioned documents, your paper will be published within 3 to 4 days.

Certificate of publication

Authors can download the certificates of each author, confirmation letter, and published paper from their AUTHOR HOME account.

What is AUTHOR HOME?

IJRAR provide the account to each corresponding author individual. Once the paper submitted to IJRAR, corresponding author got the Account credential details in author provided Email address. Authors can check his/her paper details, Authors Details and Paper status from account.

Authors will download the soft copy of the published paper certificate, confirmation letters, and Published paper pdf file from his/her account after the paper will successfully published.

Regarding reserach paper
Authors have to consider following criteria before publish the paper.
  • The manuscript submitted is author’s own original work;
  • All authors participated in the work in a substantive way and are prepared to take public responsibility for the work;
  • Corresponding (1st Author)Author was authorized by all authors to transfer all royalties related with the manuscript and to enter into a binding contract with IJRAR as detailed in this Copyright Release Form, and He/She will be responsible in the event of all disputes that have occurred and that may occur,
  • All authors have seen and approved the manuscript as submitted;
  • E-mail and street addresses of all authors have been entered into the IJRAR Journals Manuscript Submission and Evaluation System correctly,
  • The manuscript has not been published and is not being submitted or considered for publication elsewhere;
  • The text, illustrations, and any other materials included in the manuscript do not infringe upon any existing copyright or other rights of anyone.
  • uthors transfer all financial rights, especially processing, reproduction, representation, printing, distribution, and online transmittal, to IJRAR with no limitation whatsoever, If IJRAR found any copyright content or any issue related to copyright in future, then IJRAR have rights to withdraw the paper without prior notice to authors
  • All submitted papers will be sent to reviewers for a blind review. The reviewers use the following in evaluating research papers:Novel Contribution, Originality in Thought, Inferences, Key Strengths, Key Weaknesses, Areas of Improvement, Presentation/Organization of Research
